“Okay, tell me more about it”

Sure, we would love to. To give you a brief overview of how rate limiting works, it limits the number of requests that can be made in a given time period. If a user or application exceeds this limit, they may receive a warning message, be temporarily blocked, or their requests may be throttled to prevent overloading the system.
