5. Raw Data Deletion
This feature of ours enables you to delete Raw Data Lists in Single as well as Bulk. Along with this, you have an option of deleting Single Contact from the Raw Data list.

1. User Login Flow Enhancement
With this modification, whenever an Applicant/ Candidate comes via the institution’s official website, make them land directly on the institution’s login page (with the Institute name, logo & favourable icon on the login page).
2. Custom Payment Receipt &
Template Manager
Configure fee receipts and assign them to any/ every corresponding fee payment. The 3 different types of payment receipts are:
Default Receipt: In case a custom receipt is not created, a default payment receipt will be released.
Payment Receipt: This option allows you to build your own fee receipt template and map it to a specific fee type or to all fee payments.
Sanction Letter: In the case of Pay Later, the institute can create the sanction letter and map it to the specific fee type.
3. Date Upload & Custom Date Handling
Simplify how you collect the same fee from different applicants/ candidates on different dates by uploading different dates against each student and assign the same on fee connect.
