Release Alert
We strive to be better every day!

Living up to this spirit in the new year, we have added some splendid features in our product suite to help our partners strive the same. As we believe to be the catalysts in your growth, we hope that adding these features will help you optimise efficiency and achieve the bigger organisational goal.
Here are some top feature additions:
1. Opportunity Enhancements
With this new enhancement, we offer you a 360-degree view and control of the entire Opportunity segment. Now, quickly check stage-wise opportunity number and access their profile on mobile with the following advancements-
Opportunity Segment

Get a complete view of all the Opportunities in the system with a clear-cut count as per their stage/ status in the journey in the Opportunity funnel and get the advanced view of the same on Opportunity Dashlet. 

Steps to Access: Navigate to Admin Dashboard > Add Dashlet > Opportunity Funnel
Opportunity Stages

Nurturing an opportunity has now become easier with detailed Opportunity stages. Simply view all allocated Opportunities based on their stages like Hot, Cold, Warm, Closed, Enroled, etc. for all the owners assigned in the system and get one step closer to efficient nurturing. 

Steps to Access: Navigate to Admin Dashboard > Add Dashlet > Counsellor vs Opportunity Stage

 
Manage Opportunity on Mobile App
The one-view Opportunity Profile on the NoPaperForms’ Mobile app will empower you to mark follow-ups, add notes, view complete details, and re-assign Opportunity to and within the team.
2. Introducing Tabs in Lead Details

Now create multiple tabs on the lead profile and simply add other essential information in a segregated way. Quickly refer to the added information/ add quick notes in the tabs and nurture prospects on the go! 

Steps to Access: Navigate to Manage Leads > Lead Profile
3. New APIs on Developer Portal
Our team has added User APIs along with the following on the
Developer Portal.
  Create Master Data   Get Lead Details by Mobile Number
  Update Master Data   Get Lead Details by e-mail
  Get Lead Details by Lead Id
4. Check-in/Check-out is now on iOS Mobile App
Keeping efficiency optimisation at the top of what we do, this newly added feature allows your team to check-in and check-out effortlessly using the NoPaperForms on an IOS mobile. Team Managers/ Admission Heads can view each team member’s attendance.
5. Raw Data Deletion
This feature of ours enables you to delete Raw Data Lists in Single as well as Bulk. Along with this, you have an option of deleting Single Contact from the Raw Data list.
1. User Login Flow Enhancement

Now you get a 100% customised Login page (with Name, Logo, and other favourable icons) for the users/ prospects that are directly coming from the institution’s official website. 
2. Custom Payment Receipt

Make your fee receipts dynamic! Build your own or choose a certain template and map it to a specific fee type or all fee payments. In case of Pay Later, simply create the sanction letter and map it to the specific fee type.
3. Date Upload & Custom Date Handling
Map different fee collection date against each student and simplify how you collect various fee payments from multiple students on different dates.
